Our Story

Founded in 1945, Microwave Studios emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create games that bring joy and excitement to players worldwide. Just as the microwave revolutionized how people prepare food, we aim to revolutionize how people experience games.

Our Mission

Based in Kortrijk, Belgium, we've grown from a small team of passionate developers into a vibrant studio known for creating colorful, light-hearted games that capture the imagination and warm the heart.

"We believe that great games, like great food, bring people together. They create moments of joy, spark conversations, and build lasting memories."
